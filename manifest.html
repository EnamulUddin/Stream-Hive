SERIES PLOT

MANIFEST

Manifest is a supernatural drama series that follows the mysterious reappearance of Montego Air Flight 828, which vanished mid-flight and returns five and a half years later. For the passengers, no time has passed, but for the rest of the world, life has moved on. The series centers on the Stone family and other passengers as they reintegrate into society, grappling with the loss of time and the emergence of "Callings"—visions and voices guiding them toward certain actions. As they seek to understand these phenomena, they uncover a deeper, divine plan that tests their faith, relationships, and the very fabric of reality.

SEASON RECAP

SEASON 1

The inaugural season introduces the passengers of Flight 828 as they return to a world that has moved on without them. Ben Stone becomes obsessed with deciphering the Callings, believing they hold the key to understanding their ordeal. His sister, Michaela, a police officer, uses the Callings to solve crimes, while his son, Cal, exhibits a unique connection to these visions. The passengers face skepticism, government scrutiny, and personal challenges as they attempt to rebuild their lives and uncover the truth behind their disappearance.

SEASON 2

As the passengers delve deeper into the mystery, they discover the concept of a "Death Date"—the exact date they are destined to die unless they can alter their fate. Ben and Michaela intensify their efforts to understand the Callings, while Saanvi, a medical researcher, seeks scientific explanations. The season introduces new characters and heightens tensions as the passengers confront external threats and internal conflicts, all while racing against time to change their destinies.

SEASON 3

The third season escalates the stakes as the passengers uncover more about the divine nature of their experiences. The discovery of the tailfin from Flight 828 and its connection to the Callings leads to dangerous experiments. Cal disappears after touching the tailfin, returning later as a teenager with knowledge of impending doom. Tragedy strikes when Grace is killed during a kidnapping, and the passengers realize that their actions have far-reaching consequences. The season ends with the realization that their journey is part of a larger, cosmic test.

SEASON FINALE

In the final season, the passengers face their ultimate challenge as the Death Date looms. Ben mourns Grace's death and searches for his kidnapped daughter, Eden. Cal's return as a teenager brings new insights into their mission. The emergence of the 828 Registry leads to increased surveillance and discrimination against the passengers. Angelina, a fellow passenger, becomes a significant threat after absorbing the Omega Sapphire, causing catastrophic events. In the series finale, the passengers board a resurrected Flight 828 and undergo a final judgment. Those deemed worthy survive and find themselves back in 2013, given a second chance to live their lives differently. The series concludes with the passengers embracing their new beginnings, forever changed by their extraordinary journey.
